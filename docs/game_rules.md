# Volcano Rush - Game Rules

## Overview

| Parameter | Value |
|---|---|
| **Players** | 4–8 |
| **Play time** | 30–60 minutes |
| **Game type** | Strategic semi-cooperative |
| **Shared objective** | Escape the island together by building a boat |
| **Winner** | The player with the most points when the group escapes |

---

## Resources

### Personal Resources (cards in hand)
Each player holds personal resource cards and uses them to participate in missions.

| Resource | Type |
|---|---|
| Wood | Personal |
| Stone | Personal |
| Rope | Personal |

- Total deck: **60 cards** (20 of each type)
- When the deck runs out, reshuffle and reuse

### Shared Tools (camp equipment)
Shared tools belong to the entire group and can be used across multiple missions. They can become **damaged** and must be repaired before use.

| Tool | Type |
|---|---|
| Knife | Shared |
| Vessel | Shared |

---

## Characters

Each player is assigned one visible character card. Characters provide passive or active abilities that support the cooperative mission.

> Any player who participates in a mission becomes **Exhausted** and cannot participate in a mission the following round.

### Builder
Missions requiring 2+ Wood have their Wood requirement reduced by **–1 Wood**.

### Fire Starter
- Missions "Light a Fire" or "Torch": requires **–1 resource** (any type).
- Participants receive **+1 point** bonus on success for those missions (that mission only).

### Craftsman
- At the start of a round, instead of participating in a mission, the Craftsman may **repair 1 damaged tool** in camp for **1 Stone**.
- Doing so means they cannot participate in a mission this round.
- The repair completes at the end of the **following round**.
- Cannot start a repair while **Exhausted**.
- Only **1 tool** can be under repair at a time.

### Cook
In missions that use the **Vessel** tool: on success, **every participant** receives **+1 point** bonus (that mission only).

### Gatherer
- When choosing the "Gather" action: draws **2 resources** instead of 1.
- Becomes **Exhausted** afterwards.
- Cannot use this ability again while Exhausted.

### Sailor
In "Boat" missions: **draw 2 Complication cards** and choose the lesser of the two.

---

## Missions

Each round, 3 missions are available. Players select one mission, assign participants, then draw a **Complication card** to see if extra requirements apply. A mission with unmet requirements **fails**.

- On **success**: replace the mission with a new one.
- On **failure**: the mission remains available.
- Always maintain exactly **3 active missions** at end of round.

## Complication Cards

After participants are chosen for a mission, shuffle the Complication deck and draw **1 card**. Its effect applies to that mission only. When the deck runs out, reshuffle and reuse.

| Card | Effect |
|---|---|
| **Mosquito Attack** | Requires +1 Rope (for an improvised net) |
| **Wet Wood** | Requires +1 Wood if the mission requires Wood; otherwise no effect |
| **Collapsed Path** | Requires +1 Stone for reinforcement |
| **Slippery Rocks** | Requires +2 resources total (any combination) |
| **Blunt Blade** | If the mission requires the Knife and succeeds, the Knife becomes damaged afterwards |
| **Cracked Vessel** | If the mission requires the Bowl and succeeds, the Vessel becomes damaged afterwards |
| **Heat and Thirst** | Each participant must add +1 resource (any type) |
| **Night Anxiety** | Mission requires 1 additional helper who may contribute resources but receives no points; if no willing helper is available, the mission fails |
| **Camp Panic** | Each participant may contribute at most 1 card of any single resource type to the mission |
| **Calm Breeze** | No effect |
| **Clear Sky** | No effect |

> **Sailor ability:** Draw 2 Complication cards and choose which one applies.

## Volcano Cards

Volcano cards are the penalty for failing to complete a mission in a round. **Eruption** is the last card. When it is revealed, the game ends in a **loss**.

Shuffle the Volcano deck before the game, placing **Eruption** on the bottom.

| Card | Effect |
|---|---|
| **Rain and Mud** | The next chosen mission requires +1 Wood |
| **Ash in the Air** | All participants in the next mission are Exhausted for **2 rounds** instead of 1 |
| **Tremor** | Discard 1 active mission and reveal a new one |
| **Storm** | Each player loses 1 random resource (if they have any) |
| **Lava Flow** | The next mission requires +1 Rope |
| **Panic** | Next round, no more than 3 players may participate in a mission |
| **Collapse** | Every player with 3+ resources loses 1 |
| **Heat Wave** | Players who choose "Gather" next round draw **0 resources** |
| **Smoke** | The next mission gives –1 point to participants (minimum 0) |
| **Earthquake** | All currently Exhausted characters remain Exhausted for **1 additional round** |
| **Eruption** | Game over - **Loss** |

## Game Mechanics

### Setup

1. Deal **1 Character card** to each player at random, following these constraints:

   | Players | Role Assignment Rule |
   |---|---|
   | 4 | Craftsman is mandatory; remaining 3 roles drawn randomly; no repeats |
   | 5 | Craftsman is mandatory; remaining 4 roles drawn randomly; no repeats |
   | 6 | All 6 roles used exactly once |
   | 7 | All 6 roles used once + 1 role repeated (random) |
   | 8 | All 6 roles used once + 2 roles repeated (random) |
2. Each player draws **3 random resource cards**.
3. Reveal **3 Mission cards** to form the active mission pool.
4. Shuffle the **Volcano deck** (11 cards), placing **Eruption** on the bottom.

---

### Round Structure

Each round proceeds in the following order:

**Step 1 - Choose a Mission**
Players collectively choose 1 of the 3 active missions to attempt this round.

**Step 2 - Player Actions** *(each player chooses one)*
- **Participate:** Join the mission by contributing at least 1 resource card (only if not Exhausted).
- **Gather:** Draw 1 random resource card.
  - If the player is the **Gatherer** and they are not Exhausted, they may use their role ability to draw 2 instead, but will become Exhausted.

**Step 3 - Draw Complication**
After participants are declared, shuffle the Complication deck and draw 1 card. Apply its effect to this mission.

**Step 4 - Resolution**
Check whether the total resources contributed (after all modifiers) meet the mission requirements:
- **Success:** Distribute points to all participants. Apply any bonus effect.
- **Failure:** Draw 1 Volcano card and apply its effect.

**Step 5 - Exhaustion**
All players who participated in the mission (regardless of success or failure) become **Exhausted** until the end of the next round. While Exhausted, a player may only take the "Gather" action.

> Exception: missions that explicitly cancel this rule.

**Step 6 - Mission Maintenance**
- If the mission **succeeded**: remove it and reveal a new one.
- If the mission **failed**: it remains in the active pool.
- Always maintain exactly **3 active missions**.

---

### Resource Calculation for Missions

Apply modifiers in this order:

1. Base mission requirement
2. Volcano card modifiers
3. Complication card modifiers
4. Character ability modifiers
5. Players pay resources

---

### Victory and Loss

**Victory:** Collect the required number of Boat Parts before the **Eruption** Volcano card is revealed.

| Players | Boat Parts Required | Active Boat Missions |
|---|---|---|
| 4–5 | 2 | 2 randomly selected from the 4 base missions |
| 6 | 4 | All 4 base missions |
| 7–8 | 5 | All 4 base missions + Fit the Rudder |

**Loss:** The **Eruption** Volcano card is revealed.

---

### Scoring

Points are only counted if the group achieves a **collective victory**. Each participant receives the points listed on the mission card upon success.

**Tiebreaker:** If two or more players are tied on points, the player with more unused resources in hand wins.
